# Module 17 — Interview & Revision

## What You Will Find

- Beginner and intermediate Linux interview questions with answers.
- Scenario-based questions (how you'd actually solve real problems).
- Command-practice questions to test recall.
- Final revision notes covering the whole repo.

## Why This Module Matters

Knowing Linux and *demonstrating* it in interviews are different skills. This module helps you articulate answers clearly and prove hands-on ability — tuned for DevOps/Cloud/SRE/IT Ops roles.

## How to Use

- First **attempt** each question, then check the answer.
- For scenarios, speak your reasoning out loud (as in a real interview).
- Use [final revision notes](./final-revision-notes.md) the night before an interview.

## Best Practices

- Explain the *why*, not just the command.
- For scenarios, narrate a structured approach (layers, the incident loop).
- Tie answers to real experience (the labs and projects you built).
